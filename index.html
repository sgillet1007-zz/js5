About me

I am a great white shark, that lives in the southwestern Pacific ocean. I enjoy vacationing in the northern Atlantic along the mid-oceanic ridge. My favorite food is seals. My favorite movie is Jaws, and my favorite novel is Animal Farm.

Education

After graduating Suma Cum Laude from the Shark Academy of Arts and Sciences in 2008, I attended Southwest Pacific University, where I studied marine biology and received a PhD in 2013.
